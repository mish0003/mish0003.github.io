My choice based on my knowledge

  • ICT appeals to me because it allows me to combine logical reasoning, creativity, and a passion for solving problems. My background in mathematics, physics, and IT gave me a strong technical base, and I’ve enjoyed extending that knowledge by experimenting with new tools and developing projects of my own. Outside of academics, activities such as chess strengthen my ability to think several steps ahead, while sports like football, volleyball, and working out at the gym have instilled teamwork, resilience, and persistence — qualities I believe are essential in the ICT world.

My choice based on external factors

  • I found about the program through webinars, organizations such as APV and Unify, current HZ students and carefully reviewed all the information on the university’s official website. What draws me to HZ’s ICT program is its focus on practical, real-world learning. I look forward to collaborating with other students, gaining guidance from mentors, and making full use of the resources available. I’m especially curious about the potential of artificial intelligence and inspired by the idea of turning innovative concepts into real solutions through a student startup. Even though the technology sector is competitive, I see that as motivation to stay ambitious, keep learning, and push myself to grow into a capable ICT professional.
