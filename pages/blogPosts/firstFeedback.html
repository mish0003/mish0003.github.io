My first feedback

  • These first two weeks in the HBO-ICT program have been both inspiring and eye-opening. I’ve quickly discovered that learning here requires a balance between classroom instruction and independent exploration. What makes the experience even more enjoyable is the sense of community among my classmates—working together, exchanging ideas, and encouraging one another has created a positive energy that drives us forward. A particularly valuable part of this process has been looking at each other’s projects. We often review one another’s websites—sometimes without the owner even knowing—simply to gain inspiration and share knowledge. This has not only revealed issues we might have overlooked in our own work but has also led to constructive discussions that help us grow as developers. Through the feedback I’ve received, I’ve realized something important: while I do have some prior experience, there are still plenty of things for me to learn, even when it comes to the basics. This insight has encouraged me to remain open, keep improving, and see every challenge as an opportunity to develop further.
