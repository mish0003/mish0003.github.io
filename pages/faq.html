  • How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ?

  • 1. Log in to the HZ website using your HZ username and password.

    2. Click on "Choose File" and upload the document you want to print. Repeat this step if you need to add more documents.

    3. After uploading all files, check the "Advanced" option to set preferences such as double-sided or black & white printing.

    4. Select either "HZ printer" or "HZ plotter". Once the print is requested, go to the multifunctional printer, scan your HZ pass on the TouchID, and press "Print".

    5. In the menu, choose the printer you sent your request to.

    6. Printing will only start if there is enough credit on your printing account.

    7. When finished, press "Stop" on the TouchID, and then select "Logout".

  • How can you scan a document and send it to your laptop at HZ?

  • Scanning is free, but you must have at least €0.07 credit on your HZ pass to proceed with the scan.

    1. Register at the multifunctional printer by scanning your HZ pass on the TouchID.

    2. In the TouchID menu, select the option "Scanning - Scan".

    3. Place your document on the feeder or the glass plate.

    4. Press "Scan and Send" → "Scan to me" → "Yes" → then press the "Start" button.

    5. When the scan is complete, select "Start Sending".

    6. Once finished, press "Stop" on the TouchID, and then select "Logout".

  • How can I buy something (like when I sign up for the IT introduction event) on the HZ web shop?

  • 1. Go to the HZ webshop.

    2. Browse and select the item you want to purchase.

    3. Add the item to your shopping cart.

    4. Open your shopping cart to review your order.

    5. Proceed to checkout to complete your purchase.

  • How can you book a project space?

  • 1. Go to the Selfservice portal.

    2. Click on "Make a reservation" and then select "Reserve a room".

    3. Choose the room you want to book.

    4. View the available timeslots and select your preferred option.

  • What are the instructions if you want to park your car at the HZ parking lot?

  • 1. Show your HZ pass at the barriers to gain access.

    2. Once the barriers open, you may park your vehicle in the university parking lot.
