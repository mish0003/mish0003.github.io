Welcome!

My name is Ivan Mishevski, but most of my friends call me Misho. I’m 19 years old, originally from Plovdiv, Bulgaria. Here, I’ll share a bit about myself, my interests, and why I chose ICT.

Why i chose ICT?

I chose ICT because it matches my strengths in problem-solving, logical thinking, and creativity. My background in maths, physics, and IT gave me a strong foundation, and I enjoy building on it by learning new technologies and developing projects. Activities like chess sharpen my strategic thinking, while sports such as football, volleyball, and going to the gym have taught me teamwork, discipline, and perseverance — qualities that are just as important in ICT.

What excites me most about the ICT program at HZ is the practical learning approach, working with fellow students, and having access to valuable resources and mentors. I’m especially interested in exploring AI and the idea of launching a student startup to bring real solutions to life. The tech field is competitive, but I see this as motivation to stay curious, keep improving, and grow into a capable ICT professional.
